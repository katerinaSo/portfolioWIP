about me:

I was born and raised in Russia. Last 27 years I have lived in the USA. My education and activities in professional fields are very diverse. In Russia, I studied Mathematics at Yaroslavl University but later on changed my path to the creative field of theatre arts. I have become a stage/film actress and stage director. I have taught acting techniques in Boston Children's Theatre and taught visual art in an after-school program of Brookline Public School. I have finished the certificate program in Graphic Design in the School of Museum Of Fine Arts Boston and had an internship in one of the design companies on Newbury street. I always did something creative: painting, drawing, illustrating books. I have had my own health/beauty business for 5 years. I still work in my art studio with ink and rice paper and oil paints on canvas. The most recent years from November 2017 I have been learning computer science, programming languages, web development, UI/UX. As a single mother, I have raised a son who just finished high school.
